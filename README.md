## 📚 Study Tools (For Students)

🚀 New Category Added: **Study Tools** – A curated list of AI tools designed specifically to help students with summarization, note-taking, and exam preparation.  

---

### 1. Scholarcy – AI Article Summarizer
- **Description:** Summarizes research papers and academic articles in minutes.  
- **Link:** [Scholarcy](https://www.scholarcy.com/)  
- **Student Benefit:** Saves time reading long academic papers.  

---

### 2. Whisper + Notion Integration
- **Description:** Converts lecture audio into organized notes.  
- **Link:** [Whisper](https://openai.com/research/whisper)  
- **Student Benefit:** Automatic note-taking from lectures.  

---

### 3. Quillionz – Q&A Generator
- **Description:** Generates questions and answers from any text or book.  
- **Link:** [Quillionz](https://quillionz.com/)  
- **Student Benefit:** Useful for self-assessment and exam preparation.  

---

### 4. Elicit – Research Assistant
- **Description:** Helps find relevant research papers for any topic.  
- **Link:** [Elicit](https://elicit.org/)  
- **Student Benefit:** Speeds up the academic research process.  

---

### 5. ChatGPT – Essay & Assignment Helper
- **Description:** Assists in writing essays, assignments, and study materials.  
- **Link:** [ChatGPT](https://chat.openai.com/)  
- **Student Benefit:** Boosts productivity in academic writing.
